Our Achievements & History

Explore the proud history and significant milestones of JIECEP-PUPMC, showcasing our dedication and impact since establishment.

Founding of JIECEP-PUPMC

2015

Officially established as the premier organization for Electronics Engineering students on campus.

First Inter-University Robotics Competition Win

2017

Our team secured 1st place in the annual national robotics challenge, showcasing innovative design.

Successful Industry Partnership Program

2019

Launched internship and mentorship programs with leading tech companies, benefiting over 100 students.

Pioneering Virtual Workshop Series

2020

Adapted to online learning by hosting a highly successful series of virtual technical workshops during the pandemic.

Accreditation by National IECEP Chapter

2022

Received official recognition and accreditation from the national Institute of Electronics Engineers of the Philippines.

Establishment of Student Research Hub

2023

Created a dedicated hub to support student-led research projects and foster academic innovation.
